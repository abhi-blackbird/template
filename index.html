Shikshalaya Teacher Training Institute

Shikshalaya Teacher Training Institute is Affiliated by EMAX EDUCATION, AIECCE, etc. Having international accreditation and powered by Govt. Of India, MSME, MHRD Diploma in Early Childhood Education is a Diploma level education course. ... It is also abbreviated as ECCE, DECCE, DECE, DECEd and ECE. This course helps the candidate in developing the knowledge, attitudes and skills necessary for working with young children Thousands of jobs await women who accomplish this versatile training programme as only well trained NTT teachers can handle primary and junior school classes (nursery to class 5) successfully. This vocational training helps our students to become professional teachers for schools across India spanning all boards (ICSE, CBSE, State boards) and mediums (Hindi, English, Vernacular/Regional). Merit of this course is recognized by the way of appointment of hundreds of our students at un-aided public schools of repute across India. Many students seek admissions in B.Ed. after graduation. Aspirants of B.Ed. are unaware of the fact of job opportunities. About 7% vacancies come up for B. Ed. as compared to 65% for NTT. In fact, many of our students in the past have joined our NTT institute, even after B.Ed. degree, for job opportunities..

About Us

Shikshalaya Teacher Training Institute provides teacher training course in Bhopal.The main aspect of the course is to facilitate better teaching practices and improving the quality of education at the nursery level.Aspiring Nursery teachers are trained in areas such as teaching methods, childcare and health,child psychology, communication and more.Institute having well qualified professional who provide The training which help the candidates learns the techniques of molding young minds into a bright star.
